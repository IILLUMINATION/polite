Privacy Policy for Meander

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of Your information when You use the Meander mobile application (the "Application"). This policy is provided by Gleb Kopylov ("We", "Us", or "Our").

By using the Application, You agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our service to You.

a) Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Data: When you register using Google Sign-In, we collect information provided by Google, such as your full name and avatar URL. We store this along with a unique user ID from our authentication provider (Supabase).
  • User-Generated Content (UGC): We collect the content you create, upload, or post within the Application. This includes:
    • Quests: The structure and content of your quests, including nodes, text, scripts, and variables.
    • Resources: Image and audio files you upload to be used in your quests.
    • Marketplace Submissions: Quest data (including the .mnd file, preview images, screenshots) that you submit for moderation and publication.
    • Reviews, Ratings, and Votes: Text content of reviews, star ratings, and "like" or "dislike" votes you submit for quests in the Marketplace.

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Technical and Device Information: The Application locally checks your device's Android version to ensure compatibility (minimum Android 7.0). This information is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Local Log Data: The Application generates local log files (`meander_logs.txt`) on your device for troubleshooting and error reporting. These logs are not sent to us automatically. You have the option to manually export and send these logs to us for support purposes.
  • Advertising Data: We use the Yandex Mobile Ads SDK to display advertisements. This SDK may collect data such as device advertising identifiers (e.g., GAID), IP address, and device/OS information to serve relevant ads and for analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Application: To manage your account, enable quest creation and gameplay, and operate the Quest Marketplace.
  • For Personalization: To display your name and avatar in your profile and alongside your published quests and reviews.
  • For Communication: To respond to your support requests or inquiries.
  • For Moderation and Safety: To review quests submitted to the Marketplace to ensure they comply with our content policies.
  • For Analytics and Improvement: To track downloads, likes, and ratings to determine quest popularity and improve the Marketplace experience.
  • For Monetization: To display interstitial ads via Yandex Mobile Ads when downloading quests from the Marketplace.
  • To Check for Updates: The Application connects to our server (Firebase) to check if a new version is available.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Other Users: Your author name is publicly visible on any quests you publish and any reviews you write in the Marketplace.
  • With Third-Party Service Providers: We use third-party services to operate our Application:
    • Supabase: For user authentication, database storage (profiles, quests, reviews), and file storage. Supabase Privacy Policy.
    • Google: For user authentication via Google Sign-In and for backend services (Firebase) for update checks. Google Privacy Policy.
    • Yandex Advertising Network: For serving advertisements. Yandex Privacy Policy.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose Your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. User-Generated Content (UGC) Policy

The Meander Marketplace allows users to share their created quests. By submitting content, you agree to the following:

  • You are solely responsible for the content you submit.
  • You must not submit content that is illegal, obscene, defamatory, threatening, infringing on intellectual property rights, invasive of privacy, or otherwise injurious to third parties.
  • Moderation: We reserve the right, but not the obligation, to review all content submitted to the Marketplace and to remove any content that we determine, in our sole discretion, violates our policies or is otherwise objectionable.
  • Reporting Mechanism: Users can report objectionable content or user misconduct by contacting us directly at mhandy857@gmail.com. Please include a description of the content and the reason for the report.
  • Enforcement: Upon receiving a report of objectionable content, we will take appropriate action, including removing the content and/or terminating the offending user's account, within 24 hours of the report.

Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.
