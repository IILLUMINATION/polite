Account Deletion Request for "Meander"

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from the "Meander" application, please send an email request to: [mhandy857@gmail.com].

Please use the subject line "Meander Account Deletion". To expedite your request, please send the email from the address associated with the Google account you used to sign in to the application.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving and processing your request, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted from our servers (Supabase):

  • Your primary account in our authentication system.
  • Your profile data (display name, avatar URL).
  • All quests you have published to the Marketplace.
  • All reviews, ratings, likes, and dislikes you have submitted for other users' quests.

Deletion Timeline

We will process your request and delete all specified data within 30 business days of receiving your email. We will send a confirmation to your email address once the deletion process is complete.
